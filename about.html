About

Hi! I'm Joan Staite. I'm a passionate musician and audio engineer who loves the world of art and technology. I've been working in the international audio and video industry since many years, from TV, music production, Transmedia and other ambitious projects. I've studied in an institute certified by AES (World Audio Engineering Society) and Audiovisual Production at the National University of Cuyo followed by electronics local courses and other courses involved in music dictated by Berklee Online.
